About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Kalam was born in a town in Tamil Nadu state to a fishing boat owner from a once wealthy family. The youngest of five siblings, Kalam persevered with his education despite his impoverished circumstances

Achievements

  • Pioneering Scientist
  • Key Figure in Pokhran-II
  • Champion of Education and Youth
  • People's President
